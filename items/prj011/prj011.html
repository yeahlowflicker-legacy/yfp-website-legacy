Use Space Bar or Mouse Left Button to Make The Bird Fly.

Flappy Bird is a mobile game developed by dotGears in 2013. It topped the Free Apps charts in January 2014 and became a famous arcade game.

The author, Dong Nguyen, officially announced that the game will be taken down in February 8, 2014, ending the legendary milestone of Flappy Bird. Nguyen said the game was designed to play in a few minutes when players are relaxed, but the addictive nature violated his original idea.

Introduction

This is a replication of the game Flappy Bird. The game is made with Unity Engine.
