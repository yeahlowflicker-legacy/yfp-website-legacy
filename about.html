About Us

Founded in 2015, Yeahlowflicker Production is an organization that develops various types of software, including applications, video games and extensions. The organization aims to provide the highest quality and best user experience possible.

We encourage open source development, therefore some of our projects are open to the public. Full documentation, reports and API references are provided in this website.

The organization is founded with the mind of reinventing the wheel, because we believe it is one of the best ways to learn and innovate technologies.

We do not store data in your device via cookies or browser storage in this website.
